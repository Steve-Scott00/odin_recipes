Spicy Chicken Pasta

In this recipe you will learn how to cook a delicious chicken pasta dish in under 20 minutes.

For this recipe you will need the following ingredients:

  1. Fresh penne pasta
  2. 2tsp of olive oil
  3. Two whole chicken breats
  4. One tin of chopped tomatoes
  5. One onion
  6. Two red chilies
  7. 50g tomato puree
  8. 200g closed cup mushrooms
  9. One stick of celery
  10. One red pepper
  11. Salt & pepper
  1. Start by adding two tsp of olive oil to a pan. Slice the chicken and throw in. Cook on a medium heat until golden on the outside.
  2. Dice two chilies, one onion, 200g of mushrooms and one stick of celery.
  3. Empty a tin of chopped tomatoes into a saucepan. Stir in 50g of tomato puree. Simmer on a medium heat for five minutes.
  4. Add the diced veg, stir and leave to simmer for a further 10 minutes.
  5. Whilst the sauce is cooking, bring a second saucepan of water to the boil and cook the fresh penne for 3 minutes.
  6. Using a stick blender, blend the tomato sauce until smooth.
  7. mix the drained pasta and sauce together. Serve with sliced chicken on top. Season with salt and pepper to taste and enjoy.
